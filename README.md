# The Beginning of My Web Development Journey

**This blog** is just an update on my journey / path to becoming a web developer, or at least the path to accomplishing my first large project that I have in mind, and seeing that come to life (for starters). 

I am beginning my journey with ['The Odin Project'](https://www.theodinproject.com).
In my Odin repository, I will be posting what I have learned on a daily basis (as advised), but may post directly onto this *microblog* that I spun up.. I am excited to start this journey, and looking forward to sharing things that I have learned, or things that I have struggled with with resolutions that you may find useful.

**The bottom line** is that I am doing this not due to notoriety, or any type of monitory gain. I am doing this because I have wanted to. Because I was tired of saying 'One-Day' and started getting to work while saying 'Day-One'. My goal is to just do things. Period. To no longer be the one that holds myself back. 

I am grateful for the support that I have from both my wife and daughter, for without the confidence that they have in me, nothing I create would be possible.

I look forward to hearing anything that you may want to share, or to hear your thoughts and opinions after I get this site mostly functional. 

**Obviously this blog is about learning**, so it will not be perfect. I am not going to spend an absorbent amount of time with grammar correction, editing, etc. I do not know any tricks other than slightly adjusting themes and templates (yet), so don't expect anything special or to pop. This is an old-school style blog, written by an old-school engineer, in the old-school meaning of the word *Blog* (weblog). 

Welcome aboard, and I hope you come to discover this by looking for help on a topic, and that I not only was able to help--or inspire--, but was able to kill some time at work for you.
